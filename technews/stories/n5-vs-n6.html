Nexus 5

Can last-generation Nexus 5 take down this year's Nexus 6?

Old though it might be, the Nexus 5 is still an amazing phone for an amazing price. Competitors such as the newer Nexus 6 from Google bring competition, but its $649.99 price tag is not affordable for every budget. On the other side, competitors such as the iPhone 6 and 6+ can give competition, but its $699.99 price tag is ever more expensive! I belive that the Nexus 5 is still a perfectly good phone, so people should still be buying it.
